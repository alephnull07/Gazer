About Gazer

Welcome to Gazer! Our tool revolutionizes stargazing by helping users determine the best locations for optimal celestial views. Using machine learning and multiple data sources, Gazer calculates "Star Points," which rate stargazing conditions based on weather, light pollution, moon phase, and more. Whether you're an astronomy enthusiast, an aspiring astrophotographer, or simply someone who loves gazing at the stars, Gazer is here to make your stargazing experience extraordinary. Our user-friendly interface makes it easy to input your desired location and receive a detailed rating in Star Points. With Gazer, you can confidently plan your astronomical adventures, and never worry about where to go stargazing!
